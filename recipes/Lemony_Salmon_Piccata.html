Lemony Salmon Piccata

4 servings /15 mins (PREP TIME) /10 mins (COOK TIME) /358 (CAL/SERV)

Ingredients

  • 4-6 oz skinless salmon fillets
  • 1/4 tsp. Kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp. pepper
  • 3 tbsp. all-purpose flour
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1/4 c. dry white wine
  • 1/4 c. fresh lemon juice
  • lemon slices, for serving
  • 2 tbsp. capers, rinsed
  • 2 tsp. unsalted butter
  • 2 tbsp. chopped flat-leaf parsley

Nutrition (per serving)

358 calories, 33g protein, 12g carbohydrates (5g fiber), 11g fat





Directions







  • Step 1

    • Season salmon with salt and pepper, then coat in flour, shaking off excess.



  • Step 2

    • Heat oil in a large skillet on medium-high. Cook salmon until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes per side. Reduce heat to medium, add garlic, and cook, stirring, 1 minute

Remove from heat, add butter and parsley, and stir to melt, 30 seconds. Serve with lemon slices if desired.
