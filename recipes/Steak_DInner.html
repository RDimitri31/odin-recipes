Steak Dinner

4 servings /15 mins (PREP TIME) /20 mins (COOK TIME) /512 (CAL/SERV)


Ingredients

  • 1 bone-in rib-eye steak
  • 2 Tbsp canola oil
  • 1 half cup of red wine
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 1 (6 oz) package mixed mushrooms
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 cups packed baby spinach

Directions

  • Step 1

    • Preheat a cast-iron pan to high.
    (Open a few windows and crank your oven vent to highâ€”things are about to get smoky).
    Liberally season both sides of the steak with salt and pepper.
    When the pan begins to smoke, add 1 Tbsp of the canola oil and swirl.
    Add the steak and sear, flipping every minute, for a total of 6 minutes for medium rare.
    Transfer the steak to a plate

  • Step 2

    • Remove the pan from the heat, add the wine to the pan, and using a wooden spoon, stir the wine, scraping up the browned bits clinging to the pan.
    Add the butter, stir until melted, and season lightly with salt and pepper.
    Pour into a small serving dish and set aside.

  • Step 3

    • Return the pan to medium-high heat.
    Add the mushrooms and cook, stirring frequently, adding a little more oil if the shrooms are too dry, until well browned, 5 to 7 minutes.
    Spoon the mushrooms over the steak.

  • Step 4

    • Remove the pan from the heat and add the remaining oil, the garlic, and spinach.
    Cook, stirring constantly, until just wilted, about 1 minute.
    Serve alongside the steak, with the sauce on the side.

Nutrition (per serving)

512 calories, 51g protein, 3g carbohydrates (1g fiber), 34g fat
