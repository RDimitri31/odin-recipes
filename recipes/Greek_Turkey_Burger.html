Greek Turkey Burger

4 servings /10 mins (PREP TIME) /25 mins (COOK TIME) /380 (CAL/SERV)

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. lean ground white-meat turkey
  • 2 scallions, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 small zucchini, grated
  • 1/4 c. fresh mint, sliced
  • 1/2 tsp. ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. pepper
  • 2 tsp. olive oil
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/4 sweet onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tsp. olive oil
  • 1 oz. feta cheese
  • lettuce
  • whole wheat buns

Nutrition (per serving)

380 calories, 28g protein, 28g carbohydrates (5g fiber), 17g fat






Directions






  • Step 1

    • Combine lean ground white-meat turkey, scallions, garlic, zucchini, egg white, mint, ground cumin, salt, and pepper. Shape into four 1/2-inch-thick patties.



  • Step 2

    • Cook in a nonstick skillet in olive oil on medium until cooked through.



  • Step 3

    • While burgers cook, combine tomato, sweet onion, olive oil, and feta for tomato-feta relish.


  • Step 4

    • Serve on whole-wheat buns with lettuce and tomato-feta relish.
